Q42: Next Museum Visit

Project Overview:
"Next Museum Visit" is a recommendation system designed to promote the usage of Museumkaart among its 1.5 million members in the Netherlands. The aim of the project is to encourage users to visit more museums by suggesting personalized recommendations based on a combination of data from over 500 Dutch museums and user preferences.

Goals:

  • Increase the frequency of museum visits by Museumkaart holders.
  • Provide tailored museum and activity suggestions to users based on their preferences and past behavior.
  • Utilize anonymous user data and museum information to enhance recommendations.

Methodology:

The system works by analyzing the data from the Museumkaart platform, which includes the list of museums, locations, exhibitions, and activities available. It also collects anonymous user data, including their museum visit history and preferences.

By combining these data sets, the system builds personalized recommendations that are presented to users to help them discover new museums and activities that might interest them, based on their profile and historical engagement with the platform.

This recommendation is split into three categories, namely

  • Perfect matches: museums that align with the user's interests and preferences. Can include museums they have visited before, if there is a current exhibition or event that matches their profile.
  • Local spots: museums close to the users place of residence. This category give the preference to museums that are not very popular, but are close to the user's location. This is to encourage users to visit museums that they might not have considered or even known about otherwise.
  • Hidden gems: museums that are not very popular but have exhibitions or activities that match the user's interests very well. This category is designed to introduce users to lesser-known museums that they might find interesting based on their profile. These museums could be far from the user's location, but the system will still recommend them based on the user's interests.S

Technologies Used:

  • Data Processing & Recommendation Algorithm: Python, Pandas, NumPy
  • Web Framework: Flask, Bootstrap

Expected Impact:

The system will not only help increase the number of visits to museums but also promote cultural enrichment by introducing Museumkaart holders to museums they might not have considered otherwise. It will provide a more engaging experience for users and ensure that museum visits are relevant and aligned with their personal interests.

About the creators

This project was created by the Brent Brakenhoff, Pim Meulensteen, Pascal Venema and Cas Gerritsen as part of the course Data Systems Project at the University of Amsterdam.
